Sign Language Recognition App

Our Sign Language Recognition App is designed to help bridge communication gaps between deaf and hearing individuals. Using advanced machine learning techniques, the app can recognize sign language gestures in real-time.

Features:

  • Real-time sign language recognition
  • User-friendly interface
  • Ability to train the model with custom signs
  • Works offline after initial setup
